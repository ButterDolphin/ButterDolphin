Welcome to Word Work Therapy

Our goal-driven conversations will provide new insight and skills for managing negative thoughts, emotions and behaviors. We will focus on strengths and how we can use them to tear down the walls of fear.

As a therapist, I have worked with many clients who have experienced trauma, addictions, depression, anxiety and other challenges. I delight in seeing my clients let go of their past and take hold of their future. My goal is to support and empower my clients toward a brighter future that is absent of fear and filled with hope.

My Approach to Therapy

Compassionate Care

A safe, non-judgmental space where you can share your struggles and work toward healing at your own pace.

Goal-Driven

Together we'll set clear, achievable goals and develop practical strategies to overcome your challenges.

Strength-Based

We'll identify and leverage your unique strengths to build resilience and create lasting positive change.
